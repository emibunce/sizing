How to measure

You'll enter three numbers per nail, all in millimeters (mm). Round to the nearest whole number — no decimals.

Width — measure straight across the widest part of your nail, from one side wall to the other.

Arch — measure across the curved base of your nail, right where it meets your cuticle.

Length — measure from your cuticle to the tip of your natural nail (how long your nail bed is).

Do all ten, both hands — hands aren't always the same. When in doubt, round up.
