How to measure

You'll enter two numbers per nail, in millimeters (mm):

Width — lay a soft tape (or tape + ruler) across the widest part of the nail, side wall to side wall.

Arch — the measurement across the curved base of your nail where the press-on sits. (Confirm this matches the exact arch method you instruct customers to use.)

No decimals — round to the nearest whole mm. When in doubt, size up. Do all ten, both hands; hands aren't always symmetrical.
